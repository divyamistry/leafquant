LeafQuant set of MATLAB programs

There are two programs available for use at the moment: LeafQuant-VIGS and LeafQuant-T3S. Following guide shows how to use these programs. While LeafQuant-T3S is usable at the moment, it is not in its final form. Use it carefully for analysis.
